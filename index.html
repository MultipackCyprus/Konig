ABOUT KONIG GLOBAL

König Filter Paper is a global leader in producing coffee filter papers for professional filter coffee machines from renowned brands such as Bravilor Bonamat, Marco, Queen, Animo, and Bunn. Since 2016, we have expanded our expertise to include filters for various alternative coffee brewing methods: V60 cone filters, trapezoid filters for household drip coffee makers (sizes 2 and 4), Chemex filters, and round Aeropress filters. We also produce filter envelopes and sheets for outdoor fryers used in fast-food restaurants. Our paper filters are compatible with Henny Penny, Frymaster, Picto, and Boster equipment. Another key area of our production is specialized filter cloths in rolls for filtering frying oil, rinse water, and other liquids in food processing. These filter materials are used on production lines to remove solid particles such as remnants of French fries, peanuts and other foods, breadcrumbs, spices, oil film, shells, and other mechanical debris. The rolls are manufactured in the required width and length to fit specific equipment. We create filtration solutions that make production cleaner, flavors richer, and equipment performance more reliable.
